# 1) Line identity (2025 Form 1040)

- **Line 18**: `Add lines 16 and 17.`

Exact mapping:

```text
Form1040.line18 = Form1040.line16 + Form1040.line17
```

Line 18 is the **tax subtotal before nonrefundable credits on lines 19 through 21**. It is **not** the final total tax for the return (that is line 24).

# 2) Inputs

## 2.1 Direct inputs

```text

```

Backend reads:


## 2.2 What line 16 contains

`Form1040.line16` is the 2025 `Tax` line and may include:
- tax on taxable income via the line-16 method (Tax Table / Tax Computation Worksheet / QDCG / Schedule D / FEITW / Form 8615);
- Form 8814 tax (parent's election to include child interest/dividends);
- Form 4972 tax (lump-sum distribution);
- box-3 write-in taxes (`962`, `ECR`, `1291TAX`, `Form 8978`, `965INC`).

## 2.3 What line 17 contains

`Form1040.line17` comes from **Schedule 2 line 3**:

```text
Form1040.line17 = Schedule2.line3
Schedule2.line3 = Schedule2.line1z + Schedule2.line2
Schedule2.line2 = Form6251.line11
```

So line 17 can include:
- alternative minimum tax from **Form 6251**;
- Schedule 2 Part I additions to tax in **line 1z** (excess advance PTC repayment, certain clean-vehicle / Form 4255 recapture items ŌĆö most deferred).

Implementation note: backend field `additionalTaxSchedule2` represents Schedule 2 line 3 semantically. Currently equals Form 6251 line 11 only (Schedule 2 line 1z items deferred), but using the semantic field is future-safe: when any line 1z item is implemented, `computeLine18` automatically inherits the corrected value.

**G-new-5 propagation note** (per 17 #9): `wireLine17ToOutputs` currently sets `additionalTaxSchedule2 = amt` (line 11 only); the recommended follow-up fix sets `additionalTaxSchedule2 = line1z + line11`, at which point line 18 inherits the corrected value with no `computeLine18` change.

# 3) What line 18 means

Line 18 is the return's **tax-before-credits** subtotal ŌĆö the starting point for the credits section (lines 19ŌĆō21).

Before subtracting:
- **line 19** child tax credit / credit for other dependents (Schedule 8812 line 14);
- **line 20** amount from **Schedule 3 line 8** (other nonrefundable credits);
- **line 21** total nonrefundable credits = line 19 + line 20.

So line 18 is the starting point for the credits section, not the end of the tax computation.

---

# 4) What line 18 does not include (MOST IMPORTANT scope guardrail)

Line 18 does **not** include:
- **Schedule 2 Part II other taxes** (lines 4ŌĆō21 of Schedule 2 ŌĆö those flow at line 23);
- later repayment or penalty taxes that belong below line 22;
- withholding or refundable credits;
- payments.

Those items flow later through:
- **line 22** tax after nonrefundable credits = max(0, line 18 ŌłÆ line 21);
- **line 23** additional taxes from Schedule 2 line 21;
- **line 24** total tax = line 22 + line 23.

Implementation consequence:
- do **not** treat line 18 as "total tax";
- do **not** add Schedule 2 Part II amounts into line 18;
- STRUCTURALLY enforced by reading ONLY `additionalTaxSchedule2` (Schedule 2 line 3, not line 21).

# 9) Practical developer cheat sheet

```text

```

Meaning:
- **line 16** = tax line (regular tax + Form 8814 + Form 4972 + Box 3 write-ins);

Key 2025 guardrails:

- line 18 is **not total tax**;
- Schedule 2 Part II is **not** part of line 18;
- backend reads `additionalTaxSchedule2` (semantic Schedule 2 line 3) NOT `alternativeMinimumTax` (Form 6251 line 11 alone) ŌĆö G1 fix.
