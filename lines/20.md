## 1. Line Identity

**Form 1040 line 20** is:

```text

```

This is a **nonrefundable-credit carry line**. It is not entered directly.



## 4. Material 2025 Guardrails

### 4a. Line 20 is only Schedule 3 line 8

`Form 1040 line 20` is not a generic bucket. It is exactly:

```text
Schedule3.line8
```

### 4b. Line 6z is active in 2025

`Schedule 3 line 6z` is **not blank in the form design** and is **not always zero**. It is a write-in line for:

```text
Other nonrefundable credits. List type and amount.
```

Backend reads `otherPayments.otherNonrefundableCredits` and `.otherNonrefundableCreditsDescription`.

### 4c. Line 12 is fuel credit only

For 2025:

```text
Schedule3.line12 = Credit for federal tax on fuels
```

That line is sourced from **Form 4136**. It is not a miscellaneous refundable-credit catch-all.

### 4d. Section 1341 moved to line 13b, not line 12

The repayment credit under IRC ┬¦1341 belongs on:

```text
Schedule3.line13b
```

not on Schedule 3 line 12.

### 4e. Part II totals

For 2025:

```text
Schedule3.line14 = sum(line13a through line13z)
Schedule3.line15 = line9 + line10 + line11 + line12 + line14
```

`line14` is a subtotal, not the net elective payment line.

### 4f. Lines 13c and 13d exist in 2025

The current 2025 form separately includes:

- `13c` net elective payment election amount from Form 3800 Part III line 6 column (j) ŌĆö out of scope;
- `13d` deferred amount of net 965 tax liability ŌĆö not implemented.

## 5. Credit-Specific Notes Worth Preserving

### 5a. Foreign tax credit on line 1

`Schedule 3 line 1` still takes the foreign tax credit, with Form 1116 attached when required. Some taxpayers can still use the simplified exception instead of filing Form 1116. ŌÜĀ’ĖÅ Form 1116 runs AFTER `computeLine17`; its result triggers `correctLine17ForFtc` which corrects Form 6251 line 10 and refreshes line 18 (G-new-1 fix).

### 5b. Clean vehicle lines are split

The 2025 form keeps two separate clean-vehicle lines:

- `6f` for the clean vehicle credit
- `6m` for the credit for previously owned clean vehicles

Both populated by `applyForm8936ScheduleAToSchedule3`. Do not collapse them into one field.

### 5c. Form 8978 line 14 is sign-sensitive

`Schedule 3 line 6l` carries the **negative** portion of Form 8978 line 14 (BBA partnership pushout). The positive portion flows to Schedule 2 line 1z (Form 1040 line 17). Routing controlled by Schedule 3 instructions. **G2 open** ŌĆö `amountFromForm8978Line14` field exists but never populated; BLOCKED on Form 8978 BBA partnership implementation.

### 5d. Excess social security on line 11

For tax year 2025, the Social Security wage base is `$176,100`, so the maximum employee Social Security tax per person is:

```text
176100 * 6.2% = 10918.20
```

Excess withholding from multiple employers flows to Schedule 3 line 11.
