# Form 8815 — Exclusion of Interest From Series EE and I U.S. Savings Bonds Issued After 1989

Tax year: **2025**.

Authority: **IRC §135** + IRS 2025 Form 8815 + Pub. 970 chapter 10.

Form 8815 is a **conditionally-emitted output form** attached to Form 1040 when the
taxpayer cashed Series EE or Series I U.S. savings bonds (issued after 1989) in 2025
and used the proceeds for qualified higher education expenses.

## 1. Line identity

| Line | Label | IRS text |
|---|---|---|
| 1 (a) | Name of person enrolled | Name of person (you, your spouse, or your dependent) who was enrolled at or attended an eligible educational institution |
| 1 (b) | Institution name + address | Name and address of eligible educational institution |
| 2 | Qualified higher education expenses | Enter the total qualified higher education expenses you paid in 2025 for the person(s) listed in column (a) of line 1 |
| 3 | Nontaxable educational benefits | Enter the total of any nontaxable educational benefits (such as nontaxable scholarship or fellowship grants) received for 2025 |
| 4 | Line 2 − Line 3 | Subtract line 3 from line 2. If zero or less, **stop. You cannot take the exclusion** |
| 5 | Total bond proceeds cashed | Enter the total proceeds (principal and interest) from all series EE and I U.S. savings bonds issued after 1989 that you cashed during 2025 |
| 6 | Interest portion of bond proceeds | Enter the interest included on line 5 |
| 7 | Ratio of qualified expenses to bond proceeds | If line 4 ≥ line 5, enter `1.000`. Otherwise divide line 4 by line 5; enter as a decimal rounded to at least 3 places |
| 8 | Line 6 × Line 7 | Multiply line 6 by line 7 |
| 9 | Modified Adjusted Gross Income | Enter your modified adjusted gross income. See instructions / Line 9 Worksheet |
| 10 | Phaseout start threshold | Enter $99,500 if single, head of household, or qualifying surviving spouse; or $149,250 if married filing jointly |
| 11 | Line 9 − Line 10 | Subtract line 10 from line 9. If zero or less, skip line 12, enter -0- on line 13, and go to line 14 |
| 12 | Phaseout ratio | Divide line 11 by $15,000 if single, head of household, or qualifying surviving spouse; or $30,000 if married filing jointly. Enter as a decimal rounded to at least 3 places |
| 13 | Line 8 × Line 12 | Multiply line 8 by line 12 |
| 14 | Excludable savings bond interest | Subtract line 13 from line 8. Enter the result here and on Schedule B (Form 1040), line 3 |

Note (column b): the IRS box for the institution accepts both the institution name and its address in two stacked lines. The application surfaces them as two separate intake fields per row.

## 2. Formula

```text
form8815.line2  = qualifiedHigherEducationExpenses          (input)
form8815.line3  = nontaxableEducationalBenefits             (input)
form8815.line4  = max(0, line2 − line3)
if line4 == 0: stop — cannot take exclusion (form8815 not emitted)

form8815.line5  = totalSeriesEeIBondProceedsCashed          (input)
form8815.line6  = interestPortionOfBondProceeds             (input or derived from
                                                              1099-INT box 3 + Line 6
                                                              Worksheet)
form8815.line7  = (line4 ≥ line5) ? 1.000 : roundDecimal(line4 / line5, 3)
form8815.line8  = roundMoney(line6 × line7)

form8815.line9  = modifiedAdjustedGrossIncome               (input override OR
                                                              Line 9 Worksheet)
form8815.line10 = isMfj ? 149_250 : 99_500                  (2025 reference data)
form8815.line11 = max(0, line9 − line10)

if line11 == 0:
  form8815.line12 = 0
  form8815.line13 = 0
  form8815.line14 = line8                                    (full exclusion)
else:
  phaseoutRange   = isMfj ? 30_000 : 15_000                 (2025 reference data)
  if line11 >= phaseoutRange:
    form8815.line12 = 1.000
    form8815.line13 = line8
    form8815.line14 = 0                                      (fully phased out)
  else:
    form8815.line12 = roundDecimal(line11 / phaseoutRange, 3)
    form8815.line13 = roundMoney(line8 × line12)
    form8815.line14 = max(0, line8 − line13)                  (partial exclusion)
```

## 4. Decision tree

```text
1. isMfsReturn? → form8815 = null + FORM_8815_MFS_BLOCKED informational flag (IRC §135(d)(2))
2. !claimsSavingsBondExclusionForm8815? → form8815 = null
3. savingsBondExclusionAmount > 0? → form8815 = pass-through (line14 = manual; rest null)
4. Compute lines 1–14 from intake (Phase 4)
   a. line4 == 0? → form8815 = null + flag user (cannot take exclusion)
   b. otherwise → return populated Form8815
```

## 5. Worksheet computations

### Line 6 Worksheet (when bond proceeds mix paper EE + electronic EE + I bonds with prior-year interest)

```text
ws.line1 = form8815.line5
ws.line2 = paperEEFaceValueCashed                            (input)
ws.line3 = ws.line2 × 0.50
ws.line4 = electronicEEPlusPaperIFaceValueCashed             (input)
ws.line5 = ws.line3 + ws.line4
ws.line6 = ws.line1 − ws.line5

if priorYearInterestReportedAmount == null OR 0:
  form8815.line6 = ws.line6                                  (stop here)
else:
  ws.line7 = priorYearInterestReportedAmount                 (input)
  ws.line8 = ws.line6 − ws.line7
  form8815.line6 = ws.line8
```

### Line 9 Worksheet (MAGI computation)

```text
ws.line1 = scheduleB.line2TotalInterest                      (auto from Schedule B)
ws.line2 = sum of Form 1040 lines 1z, 3b, 4b, 5b, 6b, 7a
         + sum of Schedule 1 lines 1 through 7 and 9         (auto-derived)
ws.line3 = ws.line1 + ws.line2
ws.line4 = sum of Schedule 1 lines 11 through 20, 23, and 25  (auto-derived)
ws.line5 = ws.line3 − ws.line4
         + Form 2555 lines 45 + 50                            (auto if 2555 emitted)
         + Form 4563 line 15                                  (out of scope; not added)
         + employer adoption assistance excluded
         + Puerto Rico exclusion
form8815.line9 = ws.line5
```

## 7. Outputs / forms produced

- `TaxReturnComputation.form8815` (POJO) — populated when emitted; null otherwise.
- `OutForm8815` (SQL row) — persisted via `Form8815OutputMapper`.
- Schedule B Part I line 3 — line 14 of Form 8815 carried as a NEGATIVE entry on
  Schedule B reducing total interest. (Phase 4 wiring.)
- Form 1040 line 2b — reduced by form8815.line14.
- Informational flag `FORM_8815_MFS_BLOCKED` — emitted when MFS filer claims the
  exclusion (silently dropped per IRC §135(d)(2)).

## 8. Validations

| # | Invariant | Severity |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | `form8815 != null` ⟹ `claimsSavingsBondExclusionForm8815 == true` | structural |
| 2 | `isMfsReturn` ⟹ `form8815 == null` (IRC §135(d)(2)) | blocking flag |
| 3 | `line14 ≤ line8` always | structural |
| 4 | `line11 ≥ phaseoutRange` ⟹ `line14 == 0` | math |
| 5 | `line4 == 0` ⟹ `form8815 == null` (cannot take exclusion) | structural |
| 6 | `savingsBondExclusionAmount > 0 && line-by-line populated` ⟹ manual wins (override) | semantic |
| 7 | `form8815.line14 != null` ⟹ `interest.line2bTaxableInterest` reduced by line14 | downstream |

## 9. Out of scope

- Form 4563 (American Samoa exclusion) — not yet implemented; treated as $0 in MAGI.
- Coverdell ESA / QTP contributions credit for qualified expenses — line 2 instructions
  include these but the application currently doesn't have a separate intake for them.
- Form 8818 (Optional record-keeping form) — informational only; never attached.
