### Backend bug fixed during remediation

**Form 4852 line 8f federal income tax withheld was captured on the Form 4852 attachment object but NEVER added to Form 1040 line 25b** (`payments.withholding1099`). Per the Form 4852 instructions ("Be sure to claim any federal income tax withheld…"), substitute withholding must flow to line 25b the same as a real 1099-R box 4 amount. While fixing it I noticed the same class of bug for the W-2 side (line 7e → line 25a), so both were wired together.

**Fix location:** `TaxReturnComputeService.java` — two new helpers (`extractForm4852Line25aWithholding`, `extractForm4852Line25bWithholding`) plus call-site wiring in `computeLine31ThroughLine38`. MFS guard applied. Committed in `us-tax-be` `5013483`.
